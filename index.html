Welcome to Bluestar Airlines!

Bluestar Airlines, incorporated in 2021 is a virtual airline that uses OnAir to fly in a persistent, challenging economy. Bluestar maintains a very diverse fleet, and a large routes network that spans the globe. Whether you prefer rotary wing, general aviation, regional flights or intercontinental wide body aircraft, Bluestar has the fleet for you.











GGA is not affiliated with any airline. For more information or inquiries, please contact us.
